What We Do

[ Software Engineering Done Right ]

Knowledge from real-world experiences

O&B has built a reputation on hiring and training skilled software engineers. We have experience developing enterprise applications using Java, Spring, Hibernate, and other related technologies both for local and overseas clientele. Our real-world experiences are applied to our training programs. Because we are practicing engineers, we are updated on the latest on Java technology and constantly update our curriculum and materials.

The Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) uses the O&B University curriculum as part of their standard training program.
